1 /* File: uart.h
2 ** Author: M. Reek
3 ** Contributors: K. Reek, W. Carithers
4 **
5 ** Description: Definitions for the 16540/16550 compatible UART1 on the
6 ** PCs in the DSL. Mask definitions are taken from the
7 ** manual for the National Semiconductor PC87309 SuperI/O
8 ** Plug & Play Compatible Chip.
9 **
10 ** The naming convention is UAx_yyy_zzzzz. "x" is either 4 or 5
11 ** (see below), "yyy" is the name of the register to which this
12 ** value applies, and "zzzzz" is the name of the value.
13 **
14 ** Note: the UA4 prefix denotes 16540 compatible functions,
15 ** and UA5 is for 16550 extensions (primarily the FIFOs).
16 **
17 ** For many items there are two names: one short one that
18 ** matches the name in the chip manual, and another that is more
19 ** readable to use in coding.
20 **
21 ** SCCS ID: @(#)uart.h 1.2 01/27/08
22 */
